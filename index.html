About This Course

Welcome! In this course, we will explore the foundational concepts and modern applications of Information Systems. We will cover a wide range of topics to provide a comprehensive understanding of how technology drives modern business.

Key topics will include:

  • TPS (Transaction Processing Systems)
  • MIS (Management Information Systems)
  • ESS (Executive Support Systems)
  • CRM (Customer Relationship Management)
  • ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning)
  • E-Commerce
  • Hardware and Software
  • BI (Business Intelligence)
  • AI (Artificial Intelligence)
